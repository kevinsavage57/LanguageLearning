How to Play

A quick guide to all game modes, how words unlock, and how your progress is tracked.

First launch

Getting Started

The first time you open the app you'll be asked how you'd like to begin. Choose the option that fits your experience level.

⚡

Quick Start

Unlocks ~200 of the most common words immediately. All game modes and every verb tense are available from the start. Best if you have some prior experience with the language.

🌱

Start from Scratch

Begins with 25 words and Matching only. New modes and words unlock gradually as you play. Best for complete beginners who want a structured, step-by-step progression.

Playing

Game Modes

Select a mode from the dropdown in the top bar. Some modes start locked and unlock as you progress.

🔗

Matching

Five word pairs appear in two shuffled columns. Click one word on each side to match them. A correct pair disappears; a wrong guess flashes and deselects. Finish all five to advance. Words you find harder or haven't seen recently appear more often.

🔁

Review Only

The same as Matching, but limited to words you've already mastered. Good for keeping vocabulary fresh without mixing in new material.

⌨️

Typing Game Unlocks at 20 mastered

A word appears and you type its translation. Accent buttons are shown below the input for special characters. If you used Quick Start, Typing draws from all your unlocked words. If you started from scratch, it only draws from words you've already matching-mastered.

🧩

Verb Matching Unlocks later

Like Matching, but with conjugated verb forms instead of vocabulary. Match the conjugated form to its English prompt. A conjugation endings reference table is available during play.

✍️

Verb Typing Unlocks later

A conjugated verb form is shown and you type the English translation (or vice versa). Multiple valid English answers are accepted — for example, both "he did" and "he made" are correct for hizo. Note: Do not enter Spanish personal pronouns when answering a prompt asking for the Spanish translation of a conjugated verb.

🎲

Mixed Mode

The different modes will be randomly interspersed to that you are given different tasks from one of the other modes after each question is answered. Note: Do not enter Spanish personal pronouns when answering a prompt asking for the Spanish translation of a conjugated verb.

When do Verb modes unlock? Both Verb Matching and Verb Typing become available once at least one verb infinitive has been fully mastered — meaning you've reached a 4-correct streak both in Matching and Typing for that verb.
How it works

Progression & Mastery

1

Active pool of 25 words

At any time, 25 words are in active rotation. As you master words they leave the pool and new ones enter, so the game stays focused without overwhelming you.

2

Matching mastery

A word becomes matching-mastered after 4 correct matches in a row. Each time a word is mastered, one new word is automatically added to your pool.

3

Typing mastery

A word becomes typing-mastered after 4 correct typed answers in a row. For gendered nouns, you need to correctly type both the singular and plural forms. Each typing-mastered word also adds a new word to your pool.

4

Decay keeps things fresh

If you haven't practiced a word in 7 days, it becomes more likely to appear — but your streak isn't reset. Decay just nudges forgotten words back into view.

Level filter: Use the level selector (A1 → B2) to focus on a specific difficulty, or choose Verbs Only to practice verb infinitives exclusively. The pool and unlock logic respect whichever filter is active.
Conjugation modes

Verb Tenses

Verb modes start with the Present tense only. Each tense unlocks the next once you've answered it correctly 10 times across all grammatical persons. A notification appears each time a new tense opens up.

Present → Preterite → Imperfect → Future → Conditional → Present Perfect → Subjunctive → Imperative + → Imperative −
Mixed mode: Once you've unlocked two or more tenses, a Mixed option appears in the tense selector so you can practice all unlocked tenses together in the same session.
Quick Start shortcut: Choosing Quick Start at launch unlocks all tenses immediately — no need to progress through them one by one.
Your data

Saving Progress

Progress is saved automatically to your browser's local storage every time you answer a question, so nothing is lost if you close the tab.

Supported languages

Languages

Use the language selector in the top bar to switch languages at any time. Progress is tracked separately for each language within the same profile.
